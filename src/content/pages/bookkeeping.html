Our Bookkeeping Services

Professional Bookkeeping, Friendly Service

Here at Tax Chateau we aim to supercharge your business finances through our online bookkeeping services team by using the following means.

  • General Bookkeeping

    Managing day-to-day financial transactions, such as recording sales, expenses, and payments, ensuring accuracy and completeness of financial records.

  • Accounts Payable Management

    Handle vendor invoices, track due dates, and process payments to suppliers, ensuring timely payments while maintaining accurate records of payables.

  • Accounts Receivable Management

    Track customer invoices, manage billing processes, and monitor collections to ensure timely receipt of payments, improving cash flow and reducing outstanding receivables.

  • Bank Reconciliation

    Reconcile bank statements with the company's financial records, identifying discrepancies, errors, and fraudulent activities, ensuring the accuracy of the financial statements.

  • Financial Reporting

    Prepare financial reports, including income statements, balance sheets, cash flow statements, and other customized reports, providing a clear overview of the company's financial performance.

  • Payroll Processing

    Calculate employee wages, deduct applicable taxes, file statatory deductions on taxes to avoid government penalties and interest prepare pay stubs, and ensure compliance with payroll regulations, providing accurate and timely payroll processing services.

  • Tax Preparation

    Organize financial records, categorize transactions, and collaborate with accountants to prepare and file tax returns, ensuring compliance with tax laws and maximizing deductions.

  • Expense Tracking and Analysis

    Monitor and analyze business expenses, categorize them, and provide detailed expense reports, helping businesses identify cost-saving opportunities and improve profitability.
